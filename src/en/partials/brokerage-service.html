List of required documents for joining the general agreement on providing investment services to an individual resident

  • Ukrainian citizen passport or passport with contactless electronic chip (or foreign passport with permanent/temporary residence permit in Ukraine)

  • Taxpayer identification number (or passport with a mark of refusal of the taxpayer identification number)

  • Extract from the Unified State Demographic Register regarding residence registration or Certificate of Residence Registration (F-13) or Certificate of Place of Stay Registration (F-14) (added if the person has a Ukrainian passport with a contactless electronic chip)

  • Certificate of registration of an internally displaced person (if applicable)

  • Extract from the Unified State Register of Legal Entities, Individual Entrepreneurs, and Public Formations (for individual entrepreneurs)

  • Bank statement confirming the opening of a current account

  • Questionnaire completed according to the investment firm's requirements

List of required documents for joining the general agreement on providing investment services to a legal entity resident

  • Registered incorporation document and access code information (description) to the results of administrative services

  • Extract from the Unified State Register of legal entities, individual entrepreneurs, and public formations

  • Documents confirming appointment of persons authorized to act on behalf of the legal entity without a power of attorney (protocol, order)

  • Power of attorney (if acting on behalf of the legal entity by proxy)

  • Copy of passport and taxpayer identification number of the director and authorized person (certified by the owner's signature)

  • Original or copy of document containing bank account details

  • Schematic diagram of ownership structure

  • Financial statements for the last tax period

  • Licenses (if applicable)

  • Legal entity questionnaire for identification

  • Other documents and information required to verify tax residency status according to the Tax Code of Ukraine, such as tax payment certificates, passports, residence permits, immigration cards, and income and tax withholding information in Ukraine and other countries
