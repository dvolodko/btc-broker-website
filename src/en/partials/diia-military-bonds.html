Diia War Bonds

Investing through the Diia mobile app is an effective way to support our country and the Armed Forces of Ukraine during the war by purchasing War Bonds (which differ from regular government bonds)

The investment process through the Diia app is quite simple and almost identical to buying OVDP through other brokerage apps. However, Diia is not a brokerage company but serves as a convenient marketplace for investors and its partners, including licensed investment firms like the Ukrainian stock market leader "BTC Broker" (fintech "BondUA" in Diia).

How it Works:

On this marketplace in the Diia app, the investor selects the bond, the quantity, and the brokerage company through which they wish to purchase the securities.

After automatic document processing and the bond purchase transaction, the securities will be reflected in the investor's brokerage account. The investor will be notified through the Diia app and receive an email statement from the broker confirming the transaction. Typically, the entire process takes no more than a day.
