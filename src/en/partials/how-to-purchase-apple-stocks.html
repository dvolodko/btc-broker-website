How and Where to Buy Apple Stocks?

Ukrainian investors can buy Apple stocks both in Ukraine and abroad on US stock exchanges

Investment Firm "BTC Broker" has provided both opportunities to invest in the stocks of foreign issuers: as a broker on domestic stock exchanges and as a sub-broker offering access to international stock markets through a foreign partner broker for its clients.

However, after the full-scale russian invasion of Ukraine, the service of investing in foreign issuers became limited due to the current martial law legislation and corresponding NBU currency restrictions on the transfer of capital abroad.

In Ukraine, buying Apple stocks is currently possible only if an investor on the domestic market decides to sell them. As a result, the supply of American issuer securities for sale in the domestic market is almost nonexistent.
