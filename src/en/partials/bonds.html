Internal government bonds – OVDP

The best investment instrument with a 100% government guarantee and a yield higher than a bank deposit – available to citizens, businesses, and foreign investors

Currently, in the Ukrainian stock market, internal government bonds (OVDP) are divided into two types by name: OVDP and Military OVDP. The latter are the same government bonds with all standard OVDP parameters but with the prefix "Military" because they began to be issued during the war.

About 80% of investors in government bonds are Ukrainian banks and the National Bank of Ukraine (NBU). This means that the money bank depositors place in deposits is also invested by banks in OVDP, but at a higher interest rate than what is paid on deposits to their clients. From this scenario alone (yield), one can see the advantage of investing in OVDP!

In addition to high interest rates (yields), OVDP offers several other advantages:

  • No personal income tax (18%) or military tax (1.5%)

  • High liquidity for any volume

  • Quick sale (full or partial) without losing accumulated interest (unlike breaking a bank deposit)

  • Wide range of investment terms (one month and more)

  • Quick reinvestment of payments (full or partial)

  • Ability to make REPO transactions secured by OVDP (learn more here)

  • Ability to buy foreign currency OVDP with hryvnia without any current NBU restrictions
