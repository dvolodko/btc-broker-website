Consulting

The investment firm "BTC Broker" offers its current and potential clients professional consulting services related to:

  • Conducting investment research on stock markets (domestic and international)

  • Financial analysis of issuers, stocks, and bonds traded on exchanges in Ukraine and abroad

  • Assisting in the formation of an investment portfolio according to the client's request and risk profile

  • Analyzing and supporting existing investment portfolios for improvement and yield optimization

  • Providing general recommendations related to transactions with financial instruments on both exchange and over-the-counter markets in Ukraine and internationally

Our mission as a broker is to make your investment journey in the stock market more reliable, convenient, and, most importantly, profitable. We achieve this by providing our clients with the necessary tools and knowledge to reach their financial goals.

This service is suitable for both individuals investing in the Ukrainian stock market and investors who already have open brokerage accounts with foreign brokers. The service is also appropriate for both beginners and experienced investors.
